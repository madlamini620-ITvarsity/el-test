What To Expect

East London Tours will be providing transport services from East London Airport to the booked guest house, for safety reasons, we will not be disclosing the name of the guest house on this website but all information will be disclosed to our clients upon request. This tour is not for persons under the age of 18 due to the serving of alcoholic beverages during our welcome lunch and the "Tshisanyama" part of our experience. It is therefore expected of you that you adhere to the local Covid-19 regulations and carry a mask wherever you are going. Read more about Covid-19 regulations in SA here Click Here



Day One

This day is your first day with us and we know that first impressions last, that is why we re hosting a welcome lunch for you at the the guest house we are booking you in for the weekend. We would like to get to know each other and give you a taste of our traditional food with refreshments



Day Two

Do you like boat criuzes? then you will be happy to know that we arranged a local boat criuse for you. This tour will be catering for a maximum of 20 people and refreshments will be provided. Secondly, we are taking you to meet the locals and a local "Tshisanyama" (Burn the meat).. Get to understand how people from around here enjoy themselves during the weeked.
