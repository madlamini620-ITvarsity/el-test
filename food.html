EL TOURS FOOD MENU 🥞 🥞 🥕

-MAIN MEALS-

Umleqwa
- Umleqwa is a chicken with harder skin than the usual chicken. It takes around 2-3 hours to get ready, It is all worth it when you taste it's unique staste. It taste like normal chicken but with double the flavor
Assorted vegtables
- These vegetables may include spinach, sweet potatoe, betroot, chakalaka and grilled potatoes.
Red meat
- Red meat will be grilled mutton and beef, In South Africa we do not like over spicing the meat. It will be lightly spiced and tossed to the drill, sources will be served on the side.
Pap, rice and steam bread
- These are the dishes we mix gravy and meat with, take a bit of meat, veg or chakalaka. Mix it with a bit of pap, rice or steam bread.
